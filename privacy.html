Privacy Policy

Keep This — AI Notes  ·  Last updated: May 2025

Overview

Keep This is a desktop sticky notes app for Mac. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what stays on your device.

Data stored on your device

All your notes are stored locally on your Mac. We do not upload, sync, or transmit your note content to any server. Your notes never leave your device.

The following data is stored locally in the app's data folder:

— Note content, font preferences, and window positions
— Daily prediction text (generated once per day)
— App settings (launch at login, dock visibility)

AI features

Keep This uses the Claude API (by Anthropic) to power AI text editing and daily predictions. When you use an AI action, the text of your note is sent to a secure proxy server and then to Anthropic's API to generate a response.

We do not store your note content on our servers. Requests are processed in real time and not retained. Anthropic's data handling is governed by their Privacy Policy.

The Daily Prediction feature generates a short inspirational message once per day. No personal information is sent — only a prompt requesting a prediction.

Data we do not collect

Keep This does not collect:

— Your name, email, or any account information
— Device identifiers or analytics
— Crash reports or usage statistics
— Location data
— Any data from other apps on your Mac

Third-party services

Keep This uses Supabase as a secure proxy to communicate with the Claude API. Supabase does not store your note content. You can learn more at supabase.com/privacy.

Children's privacy

Keep This is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
